## Introduction

Welcome to **Gaïa's Guardian**, an action platformer where you play as Gaïa, a mystical entity born from the remnants of the natural world. Your mission is to restore balance to a world devastated by excessive technology and pollution.

### The Story

In a once lush world, nature has been replaced by industrial megastructures and labyrinthine cities. Corporations have automated the world, eradicated biodiversity, and transformed the landscape into a shell of metal and ashes.

You are **Gaïa**, the last hope of a dying world. Created by elemental forces, your goal is to revive life, destroy oppressive mechanisms, and restore the balance between technology and nature.

Time is running out. It's your turn to play!

## Commands and Controls

Gaïa's Guardian can be played with keyboard/mouse or controller.

### Keyboard

- **W / Up Arrow**: Jump (press twice for double jump)
- **A / Left Arrow**: Move left
- **D / Right Arrow**: Move right
- **S / Down Arrow**: Crouch / Slide (while moving)
- **Space**: Melee attack (Vine Whip)
- **E**: Ranged attack (Pollen Vortex)
- **Shift**: Air dash (in the air)
- **F**: Interaction / Use special ability
- **C**: Natural camouflage (when unlocked)
- **R**: Purifying water jet (when unlocked)
- **TAB**: Show/Hide interface
- **Escape**: Pause menu

### Controller

- **Left stick**: Move
- **Button A / X**: Jump (press twice for double jump)
- **Button B / Circle**: Melee attack
- **Button Y / Triangle**: Ranged attack
- **Button X / Square**: Interaction / Use special ability
- **Right trigger (R2/RT)**: Air dash
- **Left trigger (L2/LT)**: Natural camouflage
- **Start button**: Pause menu

## Character and Abilities

As Gaïa, you have several abilities that evolve throughout the game.

### Basic Movements (available from the start)

#### Walking and Running

- Use directional keys to move
- Running automatically triggers after a few seconds of walking
- A trail of leaves appears when you run

#### Jump & Double Jump

- Press the jump button for a first jump
- Press a second time in the air for the double jump
- The first jump depends on how long you press the button
- The second jump allows you to adjust your trajectory

#### Precise Stop & Quick Turn

- Release the direction or briefly press in the other direction
- Allows precise control in platform phases

### Advanced Movements (unlocked as you progress)

#### Air Dash

- Press Shift / Right trigger in the air
- Propels you quickly in one direction
- Can only be used once before touching the ground

#### Grappling Vines

- Press F / X button near an anchor point
- Allows you to hang and swing
- Can be used to attack certain enemies

#### Slide

- Press S / Down arrow while running
- Allows you to slide under obstacles
- Can be chained with a jump for more power

#### Root Jump

- Hold F / X button on the ground
- Grows a plant under your feet that propels you
- Can be chained with a double jump or air dash

#### Climbing & Wall Grip

- Move towards a wall while jumping against it
- Allows you to climb rough surfaces
- Requires energy (limited duration)

## Combat System

Combat in Gaïa's Guardian is based on a balance between melee attacks, ranged attacks, and evolving powers.

### Basic Attacks

#### Vine Whip (Melee Attack)

- Press Space / B button
- Fast, short-range attack
- Can chain multiple hits for combos
- Can break certain fragile obstacles

#### Pollen Vortex (Ranged Attack)

- Press E / Y button
- Releases a cloud of pollen that slows down enemies
- Does not cause damage but disrupts enemies
- Can temporarily disable certain electronic mechanisms

### Evolving Powers (unlocked as you progress)

#### Purifying Water Jet

- Press R / Left trigger + Y button
- Projects a water jet that dissolves pollution
- Weakens mechanical enemies
- Dissolves toxic barriers

#### Thorn Wall

- Press F / X button on the ground
- Creates a wall of plants that serves as a temporary platform
- Blocks enemy projectiles
- Can be used as a temporary climbing wall

#### Natural Camouflage

- Press C / Left trigger while standing still
- Makes you temporarily invisible to enemies
- Does not work against enemies with thermal sensors
- Consumes energy (limited duration)

#### Sylvan Blade (Gaïa's Sword)

- Press Space + Shift / B button + Right trigger
- Enables more powerful cutting attacks
- Allows you to chain long combos
- Can cut through certain resistant obstacles

## Exploration and Progression

Gaïa's Guardian offers a constantly evolving world, where your progression unlocks new areas and abilities.

### Game Structure

- Linear progression with levels to complete in a specific order
- Possibility to return to previous levels to explore new areas
- Levels visually evolve after their purification

### Hidden Areas & Collectibles

Explore every corner to discover:

- **Essence of Nature** 🌿: Scattered fragments to upgrade powers
- **Ancient Artifacts** 🏺: Objects telling fragments of the world's past
- **Anima Seeds** 🌱: Temporary boost of speed or strength
- **Soul Crystals** 💎: Game currency to unlock improvements

### World Purification

As you progress through the game, your actions transform the environment:

- Barren lands turn green again
- Toxic rivers become clear
- Wildlife comes back to life

This transformation is not just visual - it also changes gameplay by opening new passages and changing enemy behavior.

## Enemies

The world of Gaïa's Guardian is populated by corrupted machines programmed to maintain the toxic state of the world.

### Enemy Types

#### Surveillance Drones

- Small flying drones equipped with sensors
- Patrol in straight lines or circles
- Trigger an alarm if they detect you
- Easy to neutralize, but dangerous if they alert other enemies

#### Drilling Robots

- Massive robots designed to prevent nature from regenerating
- Can deploy mechanical arms to push you back
- Very resistant to conventional attacks
- Weaknesses: overheat after using the Purifying water jet

#### Mechanical Sentinels

- Armed humanoid robots
- Various attack patterns (melee, ranged shots, shields)
- Can predict your movements and dodge certain attacks
- Can be slowed down with the Pollen Vortex

#### Corrupted Bosses

- Guardians of industrial zones
- Advanced combat mechanics
- Multiple phases requiring adaptation of your strategy
- Each boss has a specific weak point to exploit

## The World of Gaïa

The universe of Gaïa's Guardian is constantly evolving, transformed by your actions.

### Polluted World

- Cold, grayish tints
- Dark and oppressive atmosphere
- Toxic mist and dilapidated buildings
- Oppressive metallic sounds
- Dark and mechanical music

### Purified World

- Bright and luminous colors
- Restored flora with bioluminescent plants
- Gradual appearance of fauna
- Natural sounds (wind, birds, water)
- Soothing music with acoustic instruments

### Interaction with the Environment

- Plants react to your passage
- Toxic waters become crossable after purification
- Mechanical structures can be reactivated to unlock paths

## Tips and Tricks

### Combat

- Use the Pollen Vortex to slow down enemies before attacking
- Combine Air Dash with a melee attack for quick strikes
- Natural Camouflage allows you to avoid difficult fights or prepare a surprise attack
- Against bosses, observe their attack patterns to identify the right time to strike

### Exploration

- Return to previous levels after obtaining new abilities
- Look for cracks in walls or unstable platforms that may hide secret areas
- Use Root Jump combined with double jump to reach significant heights
- Areas that glow slightly often indicate hidden collectibles or points of interest

### Progression

- Collect as much Essence of Nature as possible to improve your powers
- Prioritize upgrading movement abilities to facilitate exploration
- Fully purify each level to maximize your chances of finding all collectibles
- Some passages will only be accessible after unlocking specific abilities later in the game

## FAQ and Troubleshooting

### Frequently Asked Questions

#### Q: How do I save my progress?

A: The game automatically saves at each checkpoint and when moving from one level to another.

#### Q: How do I unlock new abilities?

A: Some abilities are unlocked by progressing through the story, others by collecting enough Essence of Nature.

#### Q: Can I return to previous levels?

A: Yes, you can revisit any level you have already completed from the level selection menu.

#### Q: How do I defeat corrupted bosses?

A: Each boss has a unique pattern and specific weaknesses. Observe their movements and adapt your strategy accordingly.

### Technical Issues

#### Problem: The game launches but the screen remains black

Solution: Check that your graphics card is up to date. Try launching the game in windowed mode from the launch options.

#### Problem: The controls don't respond correctly

Solution: Check the key configuration in the Options menu. If you're using a controller, make sure it's properly connected and recognized by your system.

#### Problem: The game has slowdowns

Solution: Reduce the graphics settings in the Options menu. Close background applications to free up resources.

#### Problem: The sound is out of sync or doesn't work

Solution: Check your audio settings in the Options menu. Restart the game if the problem persists.
